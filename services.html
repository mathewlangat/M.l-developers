My Process

1. Discovery & Planning

We start with a thorough consultation to understand your goals, target audience, and project requirements. This phase includes research, competitor analysis, and project planning.

2. Design & Prototyping

Based on our discoveries, I create wireframes, mockups, and interactive prototypes. You'll have the opportunity to provide feedback and request revisions.

3. Development

Once the design is approved, I begin coding your website. This phase includes frontend development, backend integration, and thorough testing.

4. Launch & Optimization

Your website goes live! I handle deployment, final optimizations, and provide training on how to manage your new site. Ongoing support ensures everything runs smoothly.

FAQs

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity. A simple website might take 2-4 weeks, while a complex web application could take 2-3 months. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you provide hosting services?

While I can recommend reliable hosting providers, I don't provide hosting services directly. I can help you set up hosting and domain registration if needed.

What if I need changes after the project is complete?

I offer post-launch support and maintenance packages. For small changes, I can accommodate them within the support plan. Larger revisions may incur additional costs.

Can you work with my existing website?

Absolutely! I can redesign, optimize, or add features to existing websites. I'll assess your current site and provide recommendations for improvements.
